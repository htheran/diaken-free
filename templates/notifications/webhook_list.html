How to Get a Webhook URL
  1. Open Microsoft Teams and navigate to the channel where you want to receive notifications
  2. Click the ••• (More options) next to the channel name
  3. Select Connectors
  4. Search for Incoming Webhook and click Configure
  5. Give your webhook a name (e.g., "Diaken Notifications")
  6. Optionally upload an image
  7. Click Create
  8. Copy the webhook URL and paste it here
